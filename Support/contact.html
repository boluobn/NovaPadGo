FAQ

1. How to restore purchases?
Use “Restore Purchases…” from the menu bar item. If prompted, sign in with your App Store account.

2. Not all apps appear after first launch?
NovaPadGo scans Applications on first launch.

3. Permission issues when removing an app?
Grant access to the folder containing the app (e.g., Applications) or remove it in Finder.

Troubleshooting

  • Relaunch the app from the menu bar.
  • Rebuild layout by changing “Columns per row” in Preferences.
  • Ensure network/App Store status is normal for purchase/restore actions.
